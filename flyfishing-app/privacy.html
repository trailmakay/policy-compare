Hatched — Privacy Policy

Effective July 8, 2026

Hatched is an offline-first fly-fishing guide. We built it to respect your privacy: there is no account, no login, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. This policy explains exactly what the app does with data.

The short version: Everything you create in Hatched — your journal, trips, favorites, and settings — stays on your device. If you enable location, it's used on your phone to sort nearby waters; your GPS coordinates are never sent to us or anyone else.

Information the app uses

  • Location (optional). If you enable it, the app reads your device's GPS to show the closest rivers and lakes and estimate drive times. This happens entirely on your device. Your precise location is not transmitted to us or any server, and is not stored anywhere except temporarily on your own device to power the "Nearby" feature. You can turn location off at any time in the app's Settings or your device settings.
  • Your content, stored on your device. Journal entries, photos you add, saved trips, favorite waters, and preferences are saved locally in your device's storage. They are not uploaded to any server and are not visible to us. Deleting the app removes them.
  • Backups you control. The app can export your journal and trips to a file that you choose to save or share. We never receive it.

What we do NOT do

  • No user accounts, logins, or profiles.
  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party trackers.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data — ever.

Third-party services

To show live conditions and directions, the app requests public data from these services. It sends only what's needed for that request (such as a river's coordinates or a city name you type) — never your identity or your GPS location:

  • USGS Water Services — live stream flow & water temperature.
  • Colorado Division of Water Resources — additional live flow data.
  • NOAA Tides & Currents — tide data for coastal waters.
  • Open-Meteo — wind/weather and place-name lookups.
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps — opened only when you tap "Directions" or "Satellite," to route you to a water. Your use of those apps is governed by their own privacy policies.
  • Netlify — hosts the app's files. Standard server logs may record basic request info (like IP address) as with any website.

Children's privacy

Hatched is a general-audience fishing app and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you control it completely. Clearing the app's data or uninstalling it permanently removes your journal, trips, favorites, cached data, and location preference.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the effective date above. Continued use of the app means you accept the current version.
